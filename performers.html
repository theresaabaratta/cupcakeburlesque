Featured Performers

Meet a few of the faces behind Velvet Sugar Burlesque. Each performer brings a different rhythm, visual style, and stage personality to the show.

Ruby Valentine
Classic Glamour

Ruby brings vintage polish, elegant pacing, and a stage presence built around confidence and control. Her acts are ideal for lounge-inspired events and polished themed showcases.

Lola Mirage
Modern Cabaret

Lola leans into bold music, upbeat audience energy, and a flashier visual style. She fits perfectly into nightlife bookings that want a lively, contemporary edge.

Opal LaRue
Theatrical Mood

Opal focuses on dramatic visuals, slow-burn storytelling, and layered costume concepts. Her performances are a strong fit for immersive or theme-heavy productions.

Cherry Luxe
Sparkle & Energy

Cherry is known for high-energy crowd connection, bright styling, and playful performance timing. She works especially well for celebratory events and pop-forward sets.

What Makes the Cast Stand Out

Different Styles, Better Shows

A strong cast should not feel repetitive. That is why each featured performer is built around a different stage identity, from soft vintage glamour to modern nightlife sparkle.

Flexible for Event Types

Some bookings need a single standout act. Others need multiple performers who can shape the energy of a full show. This roster format makes it easier to match talent to the room.

Built for Private and Public Events

Whether the booking is for a venue, private celebration, themed party, or special showcase, the cast can be arranged to fit the atmosphere and audience size.
